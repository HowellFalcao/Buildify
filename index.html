Daily Mission

Your first real
market research

🚶
Talk to 3 People Today
This is where your business becomes real. Go outside and ask actual people in your neighbourhood — not family, not close friends — if they'd pay for your idea.
  • 1
    Go outside and find 3 people in your neighbourhood — neighbours, shopkeepers, anyone nearby.
  • 2
    Ask them: "Would you pay for [your business idea]? Why or why not?"
  • 3
    Write down exactly what they say. This is your first market research — it's valuable data!
Mark as done when you've completed this task
⚡
+10 XPEarned on completion
💡 Pro tip
Don't pitch — just ask and listen. You'll learn more from one honest "no" than ten polite "yes" answers. Write down their exact words.
📝 What to note
Their exact reaction, any hesitation, what they said yes or no to, and any suggestions they offered unprompted.
